Proudest Work

Programming bootcamp

The Hague University of Applied Sciences

08.11.2018

On 03.11.2018-08.11.2018 we had a course called "Programming Bootcamp". This turned out to be a very challenging and straining class, but in the end I was very happy with my progress.

Each day we had a new assignment. We had no clue how to make it the beginning of the day, but at the end would already know. Every day we learn from 9 to 12 and after that we had to code until 5 o'clock. In the end I most liked my last days project. I made a geometric objects that keep redrawing themselves in new positions. The center piece would always be the same, but it would get aditional objects spinning/covering while pressing each of the arrow keys.
